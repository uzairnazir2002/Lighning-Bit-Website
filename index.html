About Us

Lightning Bit is a privately owned IT services company based in Peshawar, Pakistan, with sub-offices globally.

Established in 1999, the company strives for excellence in the field, offering complete IT solutions to clients in various sectors.

Lightning Bit offers consulting services and technology solutions to both government and private sectors, including specialized capabilities in system analysis and design, software application development, cross-platform integration, contract programming, documentation services, technical system design, customization, security solutions, IT consultancy, and training.
